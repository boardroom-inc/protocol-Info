## 🩳 Summary

<br />

While last week was quiet at Radicle DAO, this week we had the release of Radicle Upstream v0.2.13. Abbey Titcomb was interviewed at LisCon 2021 by Citizen Cosmos. Abbey again outlines some thoughts on how Radicle can solve the issue with participation thresholds using a delegated approach similar to MakerDAO, ENS and Gitcoin. And finally, _kdenhartog_ gives his thoughts on financing OSS development via DAO governance.
